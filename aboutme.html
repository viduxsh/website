Introduction

Hi, I'm Ivan, a software developer based in Udine, Italy. My life and workflow are driven by three fundamental pillars: cutting-edge technology, the precision of automotive engineering, and the strict discipline of physical training.

I don't just use technology—I break it down, optimize it, and build tools that solve practical problems.

Background & Mindset

Born and raised in Udine, my relationship with computing started early, tearing apart hardware and exploring software mechanics. Over the years, this curiosity naturally evolved into full-stack development, automation scripts, and server deployment.

Parallel to code, my mindset was shaped by two environments that don't allow excuses: the garage and the gym. Modifying, tracking metrics, and understanding mechanics taught me logic; the gym taught me consistency. I treat physical progression exactly like software iteration: you plan, execution must be flawless, and results are just a direct consequence of your data and efforts.

Core Goals

What drives my daily routine and focus:

  • Technical Mastery Constantly discovering architectural patterns and automating system environments.
  • Precision Driving Improving vehicle dynamics understanding and analytical driving skills.
  • Peak Performance Pushing raw strength capabilities and tracking physique optimization targets.
